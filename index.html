What we do

AKM Works is a consulting company based in Northern Virginia that specializes in providing consulting services to businesses and organizations. Our areas of expertise include e-commerce, B2B, education and technology. Email us at contact@akmworks if you’d like to connect!

Educational Tutoring

We provide 1-on-1, In-Home and Online tutoring in the local NOVA region in the following subjects: Math, English, Science, SAT/ACT and College Prep. We work with each student at their level and create individualized plans the set them up for success. Our goal is to help them implement learning skills in all aspects of their life to be effective in school and beyond.

Furniture
